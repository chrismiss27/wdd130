History

Whitewater Rafting has been guiding adenture seekers like yourself through the waters of the Colorado River for 15 years. We started as a small family taking rafting trips together. As our family grew so did our business.

Our team of expreienced guides have spent many hours on the water and in the classroom learning saftey procedures and how to handle the unpredictablity of the water. We have collectively spend 500 hours guiding tours. We love what we do.
